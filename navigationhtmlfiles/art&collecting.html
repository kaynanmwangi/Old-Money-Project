WHY COLLECT ART PIECES?

Old money families have long been stewards of culture, amassing collections of fine art that span centuries. From Renaissance paintings to modern sculptures, their galleries are a testament to their dedication to preserving beauty and history.

Tips for Starting a Collection

Focus on Quality Over Quantity

Old money collectors prioritize timeless pieces over trends—invest in a single, meaningful item rather than many lesser ones.

Research Provenance

Always verify the history of a piece; old money families value items with a story, like a painting once owned by a noble.

Preservation Tips

Store art in climate-controlled rooms to maintain its value, a practice common in old money estates.
